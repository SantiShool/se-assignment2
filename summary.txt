Agile Velocity and Burndown charts summary...
=======
Agile Velocity: The sum of completed story points per iteration, used to forecast compleation timelines.
Burndown Charts: A visual of remaining work vs. time; ideally trends downward toward zero by sprint end.
Key Takeaways: Velocity only counts DONE work; burndowns reveal scope creep, blockers, and pacing issues.
